About me

Welcome to My Portfolio

Hey there!

Hi, I’m Iara Krasnoff, a business analyst who loves finding the stories behind the numbers. My journey into analytics started with simple curiosity: I wanted to understand why things happen the way they do in organizations, and how data can help us make smarter, and more assertive decisions. With a Master’s in Information Systems Management and a Bachelor’s in Organizational Management, I’ve built a strong foundation in data analytics, cybersecurity, and IT project management. But what really drives me is the opportunity to bridge the gap between technology and people, translating complex data into insights that genuinely help teams and businesses grow.

I believe data is more than just numbers on a screen; it’s a tool for positive change. Whether I’m visualizing trends, optimizing business processes, or predicting outcomes, my focus is always on making data approachable and actionable for everyone involved-not just technical experts. I thrive on collaboration, open communication, and the challenge of turning raw information into clear stories that support better decisions.

Outside of work, you’ll find me behind a camera, exploring nature, enjoying motherhood, lifting weights, or writing and translating books. These experiences fuel my creativity and remind me to keep a balanced perspective in everything I do. If you’re looking to unlock the power of data for your business-or just want to chat about analytics in a down-to-earth way, I’d love to connect. Let’s work together to turn data into meaningful action and shape the future, one insight at a time.
